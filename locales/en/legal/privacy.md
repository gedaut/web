# Privacy Policy (GDPR)

Security and privacy are highly important to us. Currently, this website **does not collect, store, or process any of your personal data** (such as names, emails, phone numbers, or addresses).

Browsing the website is completely anonymous. Once we add registration options for tournament organizers in the future, we will collect only the bare minimum of data needed (email and password) and will inform you about our data protection measures right here in due time.
