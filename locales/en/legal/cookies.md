# Manage Cookie Preferences

This website does not use any tracking, advertising, or analytical third-party cookies (such as Google Analytics or Facebook Pixel). We do not track you across the internet.

We only use **strictly necessary technical cookies** (specifically for storing your language preference). This cookie simply remembers whether you have the website set to Czech or English so that it displays correctly upon your next visit. By law, your explicit consent is not required for using these essential cookies.
